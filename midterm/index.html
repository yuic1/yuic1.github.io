Why Topdog?

topdog is a local chain based on Berkeley and Oakland.

Born in 1966, topdog has served hotdog lovers with amazing hotdogs.

topdog has had a reputation of the second-to-none hotdog place in the bay area

and is famous for being a must-go place to bay area vistors.
